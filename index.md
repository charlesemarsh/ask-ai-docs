## How It Works

1. **Install the app** from the Shopify App Store and your store data syncs automatically.
2. **Connect data sources** like Klaviyo, Triple Whale, Gorgias, and more for deeper insights.
3. **Ask questions** in plain English through Claude Desktop, Claude Code, Cursor, or VS Code.

## Supported Data Sources

| Source | Data | Plan |
|--------|------|------|
| **Shopify** | Orders, products, customers, inventory, traffic | Free |
| **Klaviyo** | Email campaigns, flows, subscriber lists | Pro |
| **Recharge** | Subscriptions, churn, MRR | Pro |
| **Gorgias** | Support tickets, response times, CSAT | Pro |
| **Microsoft Clarity** | Session analytics, rage clicks, scroll depth | Pro |
| **YouTube** | Video analytics, transcripts, engagement | Pro |
| **Reviews.io** | Product reviews, ratings, sentiment | Pro |
| **Google Analytics 4** | Traffic, users, conversions | Business |
| **Google Search Console** | Keywords, clicks, rankings | Business |
| **Triple Whale** | Marketing attribution, ROAS | Business |
