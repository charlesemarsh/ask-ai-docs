## Integration Availability by Plan

### Free
- Shopify (orders, products, customers, inventory, abandoned checkouts, traffic)

### Pro
Includes everything in Free, plus:
- Klaviyo (email campaigns, flows, subscriber lists)
- Recharge (subscriptions, churn, MRR)
- Gorgias (support tickets, response times, CSAT)
- Microsoft Clarity (session analytics, rage clicks, scroll depth)
- YouTube (video analytics, transcripts, engagement)
- Reviews.io (product reviews, ratings, sentiment)
- Yotpo (product reviews, ratings)
- Smile.io (loyalty members, points, VIP tiers)
- Swish (wishlists, product demand)

### Business
Includes everything in Pro, plus:
- Google Analytics 4 (traffic, users, conversions)
- Google Search Console (keywords, clicks, rankings)
- Triple Whale (marketing attribution, ROAS)
- Claude API integration for automated analysis
